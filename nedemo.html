About Helping Hand

Helping Hand is a platform created by the students of GITS to help their fellow students in asking permission from their wardens and seeking maintenance of various facilities provided in the hostel. Other than this it also provides various additional features which you are free to explore.


Benefits

  • Removes the need to write an application
  • Can be used anywhere and anytime
  • Everything is online
  • Saves a lot of time for you


Features

  • Asking for permissions
  • For maintenance of facilities
  • Announcement of upcoming events

FAQ's

By when can I aspect a reply of my maintenance request?

You can aspect to get a reply within 24 hours of your request. By that time they would notify you of the time they will be coming for maintenance.

Who are the faculty members involved here?

You can check about the faculty involved on the Faculty page of the website.

Where can I ask my doubts?

You can message your doubts in Help page, you can even make any suggestions if you have there.
